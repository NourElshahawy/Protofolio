05 The Process

How I Think & Build

A creative philosophy distilled into four movements.

01

Discover

I start by listening — to the problem, the user, the context. I dig deep before I draw a single wireframe. Understanding the "why" shapes every decision.

  • User research & interviews
  • Competitive analysis
  • Problem framing
02

Design

Ideas flow to paper first, then pixels. I believe in the power of lo-fi before committing to hi-fi. Iteration is sacred, not a failure.

  • Sketching & wireframing
  • Design system creation
  • Prototype & test
03

Develop

Code that reads like prose. I write modular, documented, scalable systems — because the person who maintains it six months later might be me.

  • Component architecture
  • Performance optimization
  • Accessibility first
04

Deliver

Shipping is not the end — it's the beginning of learning. I stay involved post-launch, measure impact, and iterate based on real-world data.

  • QA & cross-browser testing
  • Analytics & monitoring
  • Ongoing improvement
